A Little About Me

I am currently a high school English teacher at West Springfield High School in FCPS and working on a second master's degree in order to earn the plus 30 pay bump. I came into GMU under the Teaching Writing and Literature program but plan to switch to Professional and Technical Writing to broaden my resume.

My family is made up of 1 husband, 2 children, 3 cats, and 2 dogs. My youngest child is a couple years away from graduating high school, and my husband and I are making plans for our future. We'd like to move south to a coastal area and work remotely. However, I do love teaching and being in a classroom with high schoolers!
